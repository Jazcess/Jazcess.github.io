Professional Background

I’m currently pursuing a PhD in Astroparticle Physics at the University of Leicester, where I focus on developing a novel photodetector configuration for use in high energy water Cherenkov detectors for gamma-ray observatories. The proposed detector design involves small area photomultiplier tubes (PMTs) coupled with wavelength shifting (WLS) plates, to increase the light capture area of the detector.
