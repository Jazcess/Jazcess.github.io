About Me

Hello! I'm Jazmin Stewart, a physicist passionate about high-energy astrophysics, photodetectors, and experimental instrumentation. I enjoy blending scientific research with personal creativity, from gaming to hiking through beautiful landscapes.

Professional Background

I’m currently pursuing a PhD in Astroparticle Physics at the University of Leicester, where I focus on photodetector technologies for the Southern Wide-field Gamma-ray Observatory (SWGO). My work includes simulations, optical instrumentation, and experimental testing of light sensors.

Featured Projects
